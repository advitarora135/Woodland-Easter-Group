About

Woodland Easter Group is a project of Woodland Pop Group made by 6 people, Advit, Anshika, Diya, Jason, Naomi, and Trevor. It is made so kids could have fun, this is our first project. The goal of this project is to have a fun easter egg hunt. The rules are simple: Try to find the golden egg. There are also going to be normal eggs in the hunt but the person to get the golden egg is going to get the most candy and the biggest prize. All eggs in this event are going to contain chocolate bunnies and marshmellows inside.

Events

1. Instructions - We are going to give out instructions on how to play. 2. Materials - We are going to provide all participants baskets so they can start. 3. Start - Finally the countdown will begin for starting the hunt. 4. Award Ceromony - Afterwards we are going to give awards to the person who found the golden egg and the person with the most eggs. 5. Afterparty - Now we are going to have a party.
